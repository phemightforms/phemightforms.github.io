Getting Started

PhemightForms is a WordPress form builder with a visual editor, ready made templates, entry storage in your own database, email notifications, payments, reports, and spam protection.

Goal: In under five minutes you can install the plugin, pick a template, and place a working form on a page.
  1. Download the plugin zip from this site.
  2. Upload and activate it in WordPress.
  3. Open PhemightForms â†’ Templates and choose a starting point.
  4. Customize fields in the builder, then save.
  5. Copy the shortcode onto any page or post.

Install and Activate

Upload method

  1. Go to Plugins â†’ Add New â†’ Upload Plugin.
  2. Choose phemightforms.zip from your computer.
  3. Click Install Now, then Activate.

Requirements

  • WordPress 5.8 or newer
  • PHP 7.4 or newer
  • A theme that outputs page content normally
Tip: After activation, look for the PhemightForms menu in your admin sidebar. That is your home base for forms, entries, payments, reports, and settings.

Create Your First Form

The fastest path is a template. Open PhemightForms â†’ Templates, search or filter by category, and click a card.

Each template opens in the builder with fields already placed. Rename labels, mark required fields, and delete anything you do not need.

Prefer a blank canvas? Choose Blank Form and drag fields in from the field library.

Using the Builder

The builder is split into a live canvas and a settings panel. Click a field to edit its label, placeholder, required state, and options.

Things you can do

  • Drag fields to reorder them
  • Duplicate fields you reuse often
  • Add page breaks for multi step layouts
  • Save often while you experiment
Remember: What you see in the builder is very close to what visitors see on the front end.

Field Types

PhemightForms ships with a wide field library covering everyday contact needs and advanced collection.

Essentials

  • Name, Email, Phone, Text, Textarea, Number, Date, Time, URL
  • Dropdown, Checkboxes, Multiple Choice
  • Address and Country fields

Advanced

  • File Upload for resumes, images, and documents
  • Rating scales and consent checkboxes
  • Signature fields for approvals
  • Payment single, multiple, select, coupon, and total fields
  • Hidden fields for tracking context

Templates

Templates are complete starter forms grouped by category: Business, Customer Service, Education, Events, Feedback, Health, Marketing, Nonprofit, Payments, Quizzes, and Registration.

Inside the plugin, every template card shows a miniature form preview. The marketing Templates page mirrors that same preview style so you can browse before installing.

After you pick one, every field remains editable. Templates are shortcuts, not locks.

Publish a Form

  1. Save your form in the builder.
  2. Copy the shortcode from the forms list.
  3. Paste it into a page, post, or block that accepts shortcodes.
  4. Preview the page on desktop and mobile.

Entry Management

Every submission is stored in your WordPress database. Open PhemightForms â†’ Entries to browse, search, star, and export.

  • Filter by form
  • Open an entry for the full field by field view
  • Export CSV when you need a spreadsheet
Privacy: Entries stay on your site. PhemightForms does not send form data to an outside PhemightForms server.

Notifications and Confirmations

Set who gets emailed when a form is submitted, and what the visitor sees afterward.

  • Admin alerts to your team inbox
  • Visitor confirmation emails
  • Thank you messages or redirects after submit

If WordPress email delivery is unreliable on your host, pair PhemightForms with an SMTP plugin so messages reach the inbox.

Payments and Invoices

Add payment fields to collect money through your forms. Track transactions and invoices from the payments screens.

  • Single price items and selectable paid options
  • Totals that update with selections
  • Automatic invoice records for completed payments

Reports

Reports help you understand which forms attract views and which ones convert into submissions.

Use the charts to spot weak forms, then shorten fields or improve your call to action.

Spam Protection and Privacy

Turn on captcha and honeypot protection so bots bounce while real people get through.

  • Captcha support
  • Invisible honeypot checks
  • Privacy controls for how long data is kept
