Installation

Live in Three Easy Steps

1

Upload the Plugin

In your WordPress dashboard go to Plugins, then Add New, then Upload Plugin, and choose the zip you just downloaded.

2

Activate It

Click Activate and PhemightForms appears in your menu. The welcome screen points you to your first form.

3

Build and Publish

Pick a template, adjust the fields, and place the form on any page. Entries start arriving in your dashboard.
