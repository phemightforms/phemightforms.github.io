The story

Your forms are more than fields

Every PhemightForms form carries labels, conditional logic, notifications, payment settings, and the quiet decisions you made in the builder. A theme switch or a botched import should not erase that work.

Website & Form Backup sits in the WordPress side menu (not the top header). After Plus is active, you open it like a vault: export everything, pull entries as CSV, or pour a JSON backup back in when you need a restore.

How Backup works

From click to calm, step by step

No terminal. No database dumps. Just a focused admin screen built for form owners.

1

Activate Plus

Subscribe in the account portal, copy your activation code, then open PhemightForms Account in WordPress and paste it (or log in). Until that moment, Backup stays locked with a clear upgrade path.

2

Open the vault

In the left WordPress sidebar, choose Website & Form Backup. You will see export, entries CSV, and import: tools that only appear for licensed sites on purpose.

3

Export the whole library

One action packages every form as JSON: titles, field configs, logic, and settings. Download it. Park it in Drive. Sleep better before a migration.

4

Pull entries as CSV

Need a spreadsheet of submissions for one form? Pick the form, export CSV, open it in Excel or Sheets. Perfect for audits, handoffs, and offline review.

5
