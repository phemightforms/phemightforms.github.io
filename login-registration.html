How it works

From license to live login page

Follow the path once. After that, every new site page is a shortcode away.

1

Activate your license

Buy Plus or Agency, open the account portal dashboard, copy your activation code, then in WordPress go to PhemightForms Account. Paste the code or log in with the same email you used on the portal.

2

Open Login & Registration

Look at the left sidebar, not the gold top header. Click Login & Registration. If Plus is inactive you will see a locked screen. If it is active, the studio opens with live demos.

3

Play with the demos

Two interactive previews show login and registration the way visitors will feel them. Use demos to align with your designer before anything goes public.

4

Create starter forms

Generate starter login and registration forms that open in the visual builder. Rename fields, add consent checkboxes, wire notifications.

5

Place the shortcodes

On any page, paste [phemightforms_login] or [phemightforms_register]. Publish. Your branded gate is live.

6

Keep iterating

Change copy in the builder, refresh the page, and the shortcode follows. No separate membership theme required.
