About Us

PhemightForms exists because collecting information on WordPress should be a joy, not a chore.

Our Story

Born From a Simple Frustration

We kept watching smart people struggle with form plugins that were either too limited or absurdly complicated. Settings buried five menus deep. Upsell banners everywhere. Data scattered across services.

So we built the plugin we wanted to use ourselves. One clean builder, one honest dashboard, and data that stays on your own site. That is PhemightForms.

What We Believe

The Values Behind Every Release

Simple Beats Clever

If a feature needs a manual to understand, we redesign it. Clarity wins every argument in our team.

Your Data Is Yours

Entries live in your database, on your server, under your control. We never route your data through our systems.

Respect for Users

No nag screens, no dark patterns, no locked doors disguised as buttons. The free plugin is genuinely useful.

Craft in the Details

We follow WordPress coding standards, test on real sites, and sweat the small stuff so you never notice it.

Feedback Shapes Us

Many of our best features started as a user suggestion. Tell us what you need and we listen for real.

Keep Improving

Every release makes the plugin a little faster, a little clearer, and a little more capable.
